# Showcase Gear Selection

Wave 38 promotes five gear assets from the weapon/armor foundation batch into
authored Visual Foundry kits:

- Fantasy Sword
- Round Shield
- Hero Helmet
- Pauldron Pair
- Chest Armor

These were selected because they cover weapon, shield, head armor, shoulder
armor, and torso armor while staying inside the current Shape Lab scope:
procedural whole-model geometry, exact controls, candidate directions, pack
review, and export evidence. Materials, UVs, rigging, animation, marketplace
publishing, and LLM generation are intentionally out of scope.

Each selected kit has:

- a real built-in Foundry fixture and catalog locks;
- a family blueprint, provider pack, style pack, compatibility matrix, control
  profile, candidate strategy, quality gate, and review manifest;
- seven novice controls or fewer;
- whole-model choice previews for silhouette and ornament controls;
- automated HQ benchmark outputs for contact sheet, required views, mesh stats,
  semantic parts, candidate survival, control visibility, export/reopen, and
  quality status.

The target automated tier is Usable. None of these kits may be marked Showcase
without a human approval marker and adversarial visual review.
