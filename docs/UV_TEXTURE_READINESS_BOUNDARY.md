# UV And Texture Readiness Boundary

Surface Lab v1 narrows the old "no UVs or textures" limitation for one
headless static-prop path only: `sci-fi-crate`.

Ready for this one package:

- deterministic UV coordinates;
- per-triangle UV/material bindings;
- procedural base-color, metallic-roughness, normal, and occlusion PNGs;
- UV layout, swatch, texture contact sheet, and coverage evidence;
- a surface-aware portable GLB containing `TEXCOORD_0`;
- deterministic headless textured preview evidence for `sci-fi-crate`;
- headless material-only Sci-Fi Crate variants that preserve frozen geometry.

Still not implemented:

- general UV unwrap for all Shape Lab assets;
- novice-facing material editing;
- textured Visual Foundry candidate previews;
- Visual Foundry material/surface candidate generation;
- part-specific surface editing;
- wear/damage variation payloads;
- GLB texture embedding;
- engine-native Unity, Unreal, or Godot packages;
- rigging, skinning, animation, or humanoid retargeting;
- marketplace or artist-approved material quality claims.

Full game-ready status remains blocked until manual review and engine/DCC import
proof are present. Surface payload readiness and game-ready readiness are
separate claims.
