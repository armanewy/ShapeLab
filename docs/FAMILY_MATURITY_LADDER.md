# Family Maturity Ladder

Date: 2026-06-29

This ladder defines how Shape Lab proves reusable authored families without
overfitting the product to one impressive model.

## Rung 0 - Primitive Family

Primitive Family proves the smallest useful authoring loop.

- simple object grammar;
- few controls;
- fast Make loop;
- visible clay variation;
- no advanced providers.

The target proof is Simple Crate Primitive v0. It must read in clay before any
surface or material presentation is considered.

## Rung 1 - Utility Family

Utility Family proves that the primitive can become a practical reusable
family.

- practical reusable family;
- semantic part groups;
- richer controls;
- provider slots.

The target proof is Utility Crate v1. It may add slots and part semantics, but
it must preserve the direct Make loop and clear clay variation.

Utility Crate v1 proof:

- body, lid, panel fields, trim bands, optional handles, latches, feet/skids,
  and detail-density providers;
- exactly seven novice controls: Proportions, Lid Style, Panel Style, Trim
  Style, Handle Style, Latch Detail, and Detail Density;
- six candidate strategies: Clean Storage Crate, Reinforced Utility Crate,
  Compact Carry Crate, Wide Supply Crate, Lidded Field Crate, and Minimal
  Workshop Crate;
- richer than Simple Crate through more semantic roles and practical detail;
- simpler than Cargo Case by omitting vents, corner guards, fastener systems,
  utility rails, label plates, hinge details, and multi-profile style bias;
- clay-only evidence in `target/utility-crate-family-v1/`.

## Rung 2 - Advanced Family

Advanced Family proves that a richer equipment grammar can still remain a
single authored family.

- Cargo Case-level equipment grammar;
- multiple profiles;
- style/provider bias;
- contact sheets.

Cargo Case belongs here and remains scoped to equipment cases only. It is not a
general archetype library.

## Rung 3 - Product Profiles

Product Profiles prove that one validated family can support curated product
entries without becoming hidden forks.

Examples include:

- Clean Utility;
- Rugged Field;
- Sci-Fi Industrial;
- etc.

Profiles may bias defaults, providers, and style language. They may not bypass
the family grammar or create new unproven archetypes.

## Rung 4 - Surface / Material

Surface and material work comes only after the clay mesh family passes its
family rung.

- only after clay mesh family passes;
- no texture masking of weak geometry.

Surface work must stay narrow and evidence-backed. Broad Surface mode, a
material editor, broad UV/texturing UI, rigging, skinning, and animation UI
remain blocked.
