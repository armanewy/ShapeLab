# Utility Crate Family v1 Report

Date: 2026-06-29

## Goal

Utility Crate v1 evolves Simple Crate into a reusable practical crate family
without jumping to Cargo Case complexity or a sci-fi profile.

## Implemented Family Target

Utility Crate includes:

- body
- lid
- simple panel fields
- trim bands
- optional simple handles
- latches
- feet/skids through internal providers
- detail density

The default parent reads as a richer practical storage crate in pure clay. The
family remains simpler than Cargo Case by omitting vents, corner guards,
fastener sets, utility rails, label plates, hinge detail, and multi-profile
style specialization.

## Controls

The profile exposes exactly seven novice-facing controls:

- Proportions
- Lid Style
- Panel Style
- Trim Style
- Handle Style
- Latch Detail
- Detail Density

Every primary control has endpoint evidence generated by
`generate_foundry_control_endpoint_visibility_report`. Topology-changing
controls use discrete product labels rather than provider IDs on the user
surface.

## Provider Coverage

- Lid Style: flat lid, raised lid, rimmed lid
- Panel Style: clean, shallow panels, framed panels
- Trim Style: none, simple band, reinforced band
- Handle Style: none, cutout grip, simple side handle
- Latch Detail: none, simple latch, double latch
- Feet: no feet, small feet, skids

Feet are deliberately internal so the family stays at seven controls. Detail
Density selects low/medium/high detail marks and also chooses the feet/skids
provider.

## Candidate Strategies

- Clean Storage Crate
- Reinforced Utility Crate
- Compact Carry Crate
- Wide Supply Crate
- Lidded Field Crate
- Minimal Workshop Crate

Automated candidate tests require at least four distinct, non-TooSubtle,
conformant whole-asset candidates and compile each returned candidate.

## Family Ladder Proof

Richer than Simple Crate:

- more roles: panel fields, handles, latches, feet/skids, detail marks;
- more providers;
- seven novice controls instead of five;
- candidates show practical crate variants beyond primitive boxes.

Simpler than Cargo Case:

- fewer roles and providers than Cargo Case;
- no vents, corner guards, utility rails, side grilles, label plates, or hinge
  detail;
- no sci-fi-specific default provider language;
- one practical style, not a multi-profile equipment-case family.
