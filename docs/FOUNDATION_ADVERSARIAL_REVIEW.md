# Foundation Adversarial Review

Wave 36 adversarial reports are deterministic JSON reports over foundation
drafts. They are review prompts and findings, not automatic approval.

The report asks:

- Is this over-abstracted?
- Does the kit need fewer controls?
- Are provider slots reusable?
- Could this become style salad?
- Are noob-facing labels clear?
- Are there too many choices for a novice?
- Are quality gates only mechanical?
- What visual contact sheets are required?
- What human review is required?
- What geometry/art ingredients are missing?
- What prevents this from becoming procedural filler?

Reports always call out that taste-bearing geometry, contact sheets, and human
review are missing until explicitly supplied. Foundation drafts stay internal
even when the structured report is clean.

Use the report as a pressure test before materializing or promoting a draft.
For Usable or Showcase targets, contact sheets and human review are mandatory;
Showcase also requires adversarial visual review.
